## Objective
Make the authored witness scene flow through the current manifest and render pipeline as the main parity path, with no need to fall back to source-image specs.

## Constraints
- Keep the pipeline spec-driven and deterministic.
- Do not move business semantics into CLI or Renderer.
- Prefer existing authored SVG/text/hand capabilities over introducing new asset modes unless a verified gap blocks the phase goal.
