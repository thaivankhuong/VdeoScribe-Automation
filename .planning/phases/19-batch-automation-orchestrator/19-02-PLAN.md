## Objective
Make scripted batch outputs auditable and retryable without breaking determinism.

Purpose: satisfy `AUT-02` by promoting the staged outputs from 19-01 into explicit per-job manifest artifacts and one deterministic aggregate status report, while making retry behavior part of the declared contract instead of hidden orchestration behavior.
Output: retry-aware batch manifest/job models, per-job manifest records, enriched aggregate status results, and deterministic fixture-backed tests for success, failure, and configured retry flows.
