## Objective
Implement deterministic timeline-to-frame conversion and event ordering policies in Engine resolvers so timeline activation results are stable at boundary frames and repeated runs.

## Constraints
- Begin only after canonical normalization pipeline in `02-01` is available.
- Keep logic in Engine resolvers; no contract bypass in CLI and no rendering/export behavior.
- Preserve deterministic ordering through explicit comparers, not incidental source ordering.
- Do not expand scope into draw/camera interpolation policies beyond timeline activation core.
