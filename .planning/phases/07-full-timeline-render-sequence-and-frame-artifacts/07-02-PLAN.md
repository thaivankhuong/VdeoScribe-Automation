## Objective
Create renderer-owned frame-artifact payloads and deterministic export packaging so every full-sequence frame becomes a user-visible artifact plus canonical manifest data.

## Constraints
- Keep artifact generation phase-scoped; do not implement playable video encoding or audio muxing here.
- Export must not reconstruct visual semantics from operations; it packages renderer output only.
- Artifact naming and manifest serialization must be deterministic across repeated runs and equivalent inputs.
- Avoid machine-specific absolute paths in deterministic keys when logical relative paths are sufficient.
- Preserve existing fail-fast behavior for missing SVG and invalid render inputs.
